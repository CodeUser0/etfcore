Why Choose ETFs for Your Portfolio?

What is an ETF?

Think of it as a basket of stocks. Instead of buying one apple, you buy the whole fruit bowl. Instant diversification with a single click, reducing your risk compared to single stocks.

Compound Growth

Dividends are reinvested automatically. Over time, this "interest on interest" effect can turn small monthly deposits into significant wealth. Our calculator helps you visualize this.

Stocks vs. ETFs

Individual stocks are exciting but risky. Core ETFs provide stability. We recommend a 90% Core (ETF) and 10% Satellite (Stock) strategy for long term success.
