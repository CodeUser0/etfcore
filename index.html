How Do ETFs Work

What is an ETF?

An ETF, or Exchange Traded Fund is a simple and easy way to get access to investment markets. It is a pre-defined basket of bonds, stocks or commodities that are wrapped into a fund and then listed onto the exchange so that everyone can use it.
